" INTIMATE SURFACES "

quoted from Margaret Law

 

Surface is superficial, sometimes unreal.

Many seemed to love viewing the surfaces, not the substances.

The minds are floating on the colors, and ignoring the underneath.

Surfaces can be splendid; treasures can be hiring behind the sight.

However, surface and substance are co-exist.

No matter they are real or unreal,

artists draw imagination through the interior and exterior.
