Since I have endeavored in learning human form in the earlier academic age,my love of human form drive me to go on choosing it as my subject matter.

Part of my concept got from my mother language¡Xthe thousand years creationof Chinese characters had always impressed me. I try to apply such culture in mysculpture creations. According to the various methods in words making, sculpturalart can be made using the similar composition¡Xby decomposing and regroupingthe segments in human form. Giving it a new appearance and content.

My works are to show thickness, energy and the strength found in Chinese calligraphy.I combine those abstract elements in a way learned from the characters to form a recognizablehuman form. Those unrecognized elements together nursed a new life to the whole entity.This is the little elements' purpose, and my purpose, too.

Since my computer modeling technique is in professional stage after working at a high-endvisual effect company for more that 9 years, I developed a new and unique way for my owncreativity. I found that inside the cyberspace there exist infinite visual matters. It extendedmy thought and view enormously. I can go through the cyberspace to collect what I want,what that ring the bell in my inner most. My intention to combine the sculptural art and computertechnology to a new art creation is achieved.
